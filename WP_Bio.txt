William Primett is a composer and sound artist based in Tallinn via dance-technology research group, MODINA, taking interactive technologies at the core of contemporary stage practice. Co-founder and resident of Sensory \ Underload, offering a softer appeal to the local electronic music scene. Their ambient works fuse together tender soundscapes with fragile textures, composed from a custom electronic setup alongside algorithmically generated rhythms, forming a playground for sound and movement. William holds a PhD in Biomedical Engineering in partnership with PLUX Wireless Biosignals, working closely with embodied sensor technologies and creative programming, fostering a unique range of hardware tools for contemporary dance performance and sound, contributing to the thesis “Non-Verbal Communication with Physiological Sensors: The Aesthetic Domain of Wearables and Neural Networks” (2023)
